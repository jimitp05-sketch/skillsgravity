# 👑 The Anti-Gravity CEO Playbook (v1.0)

> "This is not generic leadership. This is the art of bending reality through systemized execution."

This playbook provides the step-by-step cognitive framework for a founder to operate at the "Anti-Gravity" level, integrating AI across every business dimension.

---

## 🧭 Phase 1: Strategic Thinking (The Vision)

### 1. Problem Decomposition
- **Goal**: Break a "Messy" business problem into 10% components.
- **Skill**: `brainstorming` + `strategic-compact`.
- **CEO Action**: Use the 5-Module framework to identify if the solution lies in **Coding** (Product), **Surfing** (Insight), **Marketing** (Growth), or **SEO** (Asset).

### 2. First-Principles Anchoring
- **Goal**: Avoid "Cargo Cult" business decisions.
- **CEO Action**: Strip the problem to its fundamental truths. (e.g., "Do we need a sales team, or do we need a viral loop in our `anti-gravity-marketing` module?")

---

## 🚀 Phase 2: Systematic Execution (The Pulse)

### 1. The Agentic Master Workflow
Every major decision or feature must follow the `Major Orchestrator` protocol:
1.  **Brainstorm**: Recursive inquiry for 100% clarity.
2.  **Plan**: Atomic 2-5 min tasks (The "Elite" Circuit).
3.  **Execute**: Subagent clusters with TDD verification.
4.  **Verify**: Dual-audit gate (Spec vs. Quality).

### 2. Information Intelligence (The Surface)
- **CEO Action**: Never make a decision without a `gap-analysis` and `competitor-research` sweep using the `anti-gravity-surfing` tools.
- **Goal**: Find the "Unfair Advantage" before the first line of code is written.

---

## 📈 Phase 3: Scaling & Leverage (The Gravity)

### 1. Build Assets, Not Expenses
- **Coding**: Build a `tools/` library of reusable MCP servers.
- **SEO**: Build a `programmatic-seo` pipeline for topical authority.
- **Marketing**: Build `viral-loops` that grow while you sleep.

### 2. The Decision Audit
- **Goal**: Move from "Reactive" to "Probabilistic" decision making.
- **CEO Action**: Log all major decisions in `/experiments`. Audit the "Result vs. Prediction" weekly.

---

## 🧘 Phase 4: Internal Strength (The Core)

### 1. Handling Uncertainty
- **CEO Action**: Use the `systematic-debugging` principles for business failures. Identify the root cause, not the symptom.
- **Skill**: `pi-superpowers` + `emotional-control`.

### 2. Delegation to the Swarm
- **Goal**: If it's repetitive, it must be an Agent.
- **Action**: Update the `Skill Engine` whenever you find a new recurring pattern.
